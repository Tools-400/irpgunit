iRPGUnit USer Space Layout V2
=============================

Header:

+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
| Offset | Type       | Field                               |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|      0 | INT(4)     | Total size of the user space.       |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|      4 | INT(4)     | Version number.                     |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|      8 | CHAR(20)   | Qualified name of the test suite.   |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     28 | INT(4)     | Number of runs.                     |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     32 | INT(4)     | Number of assertions.               |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     36 | INT(4)     | Number of failures.                 |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     40 | INT(4)     | Number of errors.                   |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     44 | INT(4)     | Offset test cases. 1)               |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     48 | INT(4)     | Number of test cases.               |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     52 | CHAR(10)   | System name.                        |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     62 | CHAR(10)   | Name of the spooled file.           |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     72 | INT(4)     | Spooled file number.                |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     76 | CHAR(10)   | Job name.                           |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     86 | CHAR(10)   | Job user.                           |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     96 | CHAR(6)    | Job number.                         |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|    102 | CHAR(30)   | Qualified source member name.       |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|    132 | INT(4)     | Number of test cases returned.      |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|    136 | CHAR(120)  | Reserved.                           |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
| Test cases V4                                             |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
Total length: 256

Test Case V4:

+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|      0 | INT(4)     | Total length of the test case entry.|
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|      4 | CHAR(1)    | Test result.                        |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|      5 | CHAR(11)   | Reserved.                           |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     16 | INT(4)     | Number of assertions.               |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     20 | INT(4)     | Number of call stack entries.       |
|        |            | Only for entry type: Failure        |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     24 | INT(4)     | Offset call stack entries. 1)       |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     28 | INT(4)     | Offset next call stack entry. 1)    |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     32 | INT(2)     | Length of test case entry.          |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     34 | INT(2)     | Length of exception message.        |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     36 | CHAR(100)  | Test case.                          |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|    136 | CHAR(1024) | Exception message.                  |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|   1160 | INT(4)     | Offset sender information. 1)       |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|   1160 | INT(2)     | Length of sender information.       |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|   1160 | INT(4)     | Offset receiver information. 1)     |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|   1162 | INT(2)     | Length of receiver information.     |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
| Execution time                                            |
| Sender information                                        |
| Receiver information                                      |
| Call stack entries                                        |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
Total length: 1164

Sender information:
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|      0 | CHAR(20)   | Qualified program name.             |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     20 | CHAR(20)   | Qualified module name.              |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     40 | CHAR(10)   | Specification (line) number.        |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     50 | INT(2)     | Length of procedure name.           |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     52 | CHAR(258)  | Procedure name.                     |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
Total length: 308

1) Offsets start at the beginning of the user space at offset 0.

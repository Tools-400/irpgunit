iRPGUnit USer Space Layout V4
=============================

Header:

+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
| Offset | Type       | Field                               |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|      0 | INT(4)     | Total size of the user space.       |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|      4 | INT(4)     | Version number.                     |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|      8 | CHAR(20)   | Qualified name of the test suite.   |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     28 | INT(4)     | Number of runs.                     |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     32 | INT(4)     | Number of assertions.               |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     36 | INT(4)     | Number of failures.                 |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     40 | INT(4)     | Number of errors.                   |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     44 | INT(4)     | Offset test cases. 1)               |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     48 | INT(4)     | Number of test cases.               |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     52 | CHAR(10)   | System name.                        |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     62 | CHAR(10)   | Name of the spooled file.           |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     72 | INT(4)     | Spooled file number.                |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     76 | CHAR(10)   | Job name.                           |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     86 | CHAR(10)   | Job user.                           |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     96 | CHAR(6)    | Job number.                         |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|    102 | CHAR(30)   | Qualified source member name.       |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|    132 | INT(4)     | Number of test cases returned.      |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|    136 | CHAR(120)  | Reserved.                           |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|        | CHAR(*)    | Test cases V4                       |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
Total length: 256

Test Case V4:

+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|      0 | INT(4)     | Offset next test case entry. 1)     |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|      4 | INT(2)     | Total length of the test case entry.|
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|      6 | CHAR(1)    | Test result.                        |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|      7 | CHAR(1)    | Reserved.                           |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|      8 | INT(4)     | Number of assertions.               |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     12 | INT(8)     | Execution time in milliseconds.     |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     20 | INT(4)     | Offset test case text. 1)           |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     24 | INT(2)     | Length of test case text.           |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     26 | INT(4)     | Offset exception message.           |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     30 | INT(2)     | Length of exception message.        |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     32 | INT(4)     | Offset of sender information.       |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     36 | INT(2)     | Length of sender information.       |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     38 | INT(4)     | Offset of receiver information.     |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     42 | INT(2)     | Length of receiver information.     |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     44 | INT(4)     | Offset first call stack entry. 1)   |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     48 | INT(2)     | Number of call stack entries.       |
|        |            | Only for entry type: Failure        |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|        | CHAR(*)    | Test case text.                     |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|        | CHAR(*)    | Exception message.                  |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|        | CHAR(*)    | Sender information.                 |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|        | CHAR(*)    | Receiver information.               |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|        | CHAR(*)    | Call stack entries.                 |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
Total length: 50

Sender/receiver information V4:

+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|      0 | INT(2)     | Total entry length.                 |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|      2 | CHAR(20)   | Qualified program name.             |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     22 | CHAR(20)   | Qualified module name.              |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     42 | CHAR(10)   | Specification (line) number.        |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     52 | INT(4)     | Offset procedure name.              |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     56 | INT(2)     | Length of procedure name.           |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|        | CHAR(*)    | Procedure name.                     |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
Total length: 58

Call stack entry V4:

+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|      0 | INT(4)     | Offset next call stack entry. 1)    |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|      4 | INT(2)     | Total length of call stack entry.   |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|      6 | CHAR(20)   | Qualified program name.             |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     26 | CHAR(20)   | Qualified module name.              |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     46 | CHAR(10)   | Specification (line) number.        |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     56 | INT(4)     | Offset procedure name.              |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     60 | INT(2)     | Length of procedure name.           |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|     62 | CHAR(30)   | Qualified source member.            |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
|        | CHAR(*)    | Procedure name.                     |
+--------+--------------------------------------------------+
Total length: 92


1) Offsets start at the beginning of the user space at offset 0.
